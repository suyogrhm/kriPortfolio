HAMSAK

We live in an advanced world where a person needs to commute a lot for their livelihood. We are used to travelling with the help of an IC engine which is harmful to the environment. Off late, there is a transition where users opt for a vehicle with an electric motor rather than one with an IC engine. This created curiosity among us designers to come up with a new electric vehicle design to attract younger users, leading to the creation of a cleaner environment.

Existing electric vehicles tend to embody either a rugged or cute aesthetic, but rarely manage to seamlessly combine both. Typically, users are drawn to a product based on their initial visual impressions.

User Study Research

There are so many vehicles that exist in the market that have been able to attract the users over the years. The agenda was to understand the needs of our end users. In this journey, we understood that there are many factors that influence the users decisions.

We have conducted two surveys- an ethnography study and questionnaire survey. The objective was to understand the users and come up with a product that solves their pain-points.

Research Findings

We circulated certain questionnaires among the possible users via Google Forms. Additionally, we conducted interviews with existing EV owners to understand what are the problems with existing vehicles.

As a result, we understood that there is an opportunity for us to create a moped with a cute and rugged aesthetic at the same time in the Electric Vehicle segment. The main purpose is to attract the youngsters and inspire them to move towards electric with an aesthetic and ergonomically designed EV.
