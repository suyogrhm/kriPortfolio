Signal

We used to ask people for directions to reach our destination Now, with technological advancements in place, we depend on our smart devices. Most bikes above 300cc come with a TFT (Thin-Film Transistor). But, the necessity of the display has increased. With the help of this product, you can customize your vehicle, which in return will make your day-to-day travel easier. This product enhances your vehicle display visibility for a safer ride.

This product is connected to your phone. It also has the capability of giving directions, which reduces the pain point of checking your phone frequently for directions

Research Findings

The number of accidents caused by the usage of phones while riding has increased significantly. It is also not feasible to stop our ride to look into our phone for direction. We live in a world where we travel to unknown places with the help of maps on our smartphones.

This has created a platform for two-wheeler manufacturing companies to introduce TFT displays on their higher-end vehicles. This product is meant for users who like to have a customized TFT display screen and for users who want seamless rides.

The product is supposed to replace the current display or ODO meter. The display is directly connected to the ECU of the vehicle. Signal believes the product needs to be as simple as possible hence, it is designed as a plug-and-play. To unveil more features of the display you need to install the app from the App Store.
