Universal OTT

OTT (Over-The-Top) platforms were created out of the intention for users to consume content in various devices at any given point of time. These platforms have gained popularity over the past couple of years as they provide flexibility in watching any specific content on any device at any time.

Challenges

With the increase in popularity of OTT platforms there have been many numerous platforms that have been created. These platforms were supposed to be a replacement for our traditional Television and are supposed make consuming content more easier. During my research the pain points that I have come a cross are

  • Owning multiple platforms and paying an insane
    amount of money
  • Indefinite amount of time spent browsing and
    searching for movies on various platforms
  • Despite not being able to
    find the movies/shows to watch

My Role as a Designer

As a designer, my role is to address the pain points faced by users in the OTT platform space involves:

  • Empathising with User Needs
  • Analysing Pain Points
  • Design Thinking for Solutions
  • Iterative Prototyping
  • Usability and Accessibility
  • Collaboration with Stakeholders

Discovery

The Tata Play Binge bundle currently lacks the inclusion of key platforms such as Netflix and Amazon Prime. This omission is a significant drawback, as these platforms host popular shows and movies essential for attracting a diverse target audience. To optimize the bundle's appeal and compete effectively, it is recommended to consider incorporating Netflix and Amazon Prime into the subscription plans. Additionally, the bundle faces a challenge in terms of awareness, given its limited visibility on popular search and review platforms such as Google, YouTube, and IMDb. Improving visibility on these widely-used platforms is crucial for attracting and informing potential subscribers and enhancing the overall success of the Tata Play Binge bundle.

Future steps

In the future, the development of the SDK Kit for Google for the universal OTT platform will focus on integrating user feedback to enhance user experience and satisfaction. I plan to implement continuous integration and deployment practices for streamlined updates. Exploring AI and machine learning integration aims to improve content recommendations and analytics. Expanding platform compatibility, including strategic partnerships, will ensure broader reach and accessibility. Additionally, I will explore advanced monetisation features, address evolving cybersecurity measures, and adapt to global audience needs through localisation. Ongoing education programs for developers, staying abreast of industry trends, providing advanced data analytics, and ensuring regulatory compliance will be integral to the continued success and relevance of our SDK Kit.
