Coherence

Fitness trackers have become important devices in every fitness enthusiast's life. Fitness is not only for just the enthusiasts but for everyone to lead a healthy life. A fitness tracker not only measures your footsteps or activities but also your heart rate and calories. This is a niche market and there are certain reliable companies (Apple, Samsung, Mi, Gramin) that cater to the requirements for the age group between 20 - 55. However, no brand caters to seniors.

This venture is an effort to address the product to be designed especially for seniors. Additionally, it is designed so that one can be aware of a senior citizen's well being with few clicks.
