IMDb Case Study

The website works like a CV or resume of the cast & production crew. It has about 3 million data base about movies, & TV series with over 200 million new viewers every month. IMDb has two types of revenue.

  • Subscription-based Revenue
  • Advertisement-based Revenue

IMDb's main competitors are Netflix, Youtube, Rottentomato and Hulu. These websites all have a common purpose of sharing information or experiences in various forms. This information can be contributed by the staff or the fans. IMDb is known for its website's data, which is not accessible to everyone. The data may have the contributions of certain fans, but it is uploaded only after vigorous authentications by the AI system and staff of IMDb. Hence, the database produced by IMDb is both vast and accurate, whereas the information contributed by its competitors is not so.

The common problem that we all face is finding a movie to watch on the right OTT platform, both of which can be solved by IMDb.

What if we can make things even better?

Even though IMDb is owned by Amazon, it functions as a neutral party to Netflix, Hotstar and Prime Video. What if IMDb can come up with a subscription plan where you can access any movie from any OTT platform with just one bill to be paid to IMDb?

Design Basic Index - Jim Krause

If a web surfer loads a page that appears before a user as a disorderly and daunting wall of content, they are likely to perceive the site as being of suspect integrity and its product of dubious quality, or they will simply leave the site. An orderly representation of visual dominance between elements not only helps attract attention to a section but, also serves to guide the viewer's eye through its content. Elements can dominate through relative size, comparison of colour or an intriguing presentation. As a designer, it's up to you to act as a referee between competing elements and decide which ones will dominate and how. Avoid a fainthearted approach while making these decisions.

Value Proposition

The need for information about the series is to want to entertain yourself by watching it. The information about the platform where it is available makes it effortless for the users to navigate to the specific page.

The video in the background is end-to-end which will start playing as soon as you land on this page, which helps us in gaining the viewers attention. The trailer video is going to give them a small brief about the series which helps them make an informed decision about wanting to watch the series.

Social Proof
