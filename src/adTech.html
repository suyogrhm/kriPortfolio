Journey at GreedyGame

During my tenure at GreedyGame, I had the privilege to work as a UI Designer within the realm of Adtech. GreedyGame is a Google Channel Partner, which provided me a stimulating environment to innovate and collaborate across different teams.

Challenges

We identified a significant gap between our brand identity and the individual products offered on our website. Despite each product having its own standalone presence, there was an inconsistency in how our brand was represented across these offerings, hindering our ability to convey our message. Our existing website lacked refinements in various areas- design trends, and educating the users. We also sought to implement the best practices followed by the industry giants. Our previous website failed to provide a better user experience due to its unstructured approach.

My Role as a Designer

As a designer, my role is to going into the depth of the research on the existing product, what is the end goal to reach and to execute it. Some of the guidelines that I set for myself before starting this project are:

  1. Differentiate the main website from the product
    website to establish clear brand identities.
  2. Analyse competitors' websites to identify both
    ineffective elements and successful strategies and
    refine our website design.
  3. Optimise the product page to serve as a source of
    information about our dashboard's functionality,
    features, and benefits to users.
  4. Create a clear and intuitive information
    architecture that priorities user needs for a better
    experience.
  5. Implement visually captivating elements
    strategically throughout the website to captivate users'
    attention and encourage engagement.

Discovery

To address the challenges, we conceptualised and developed a new platform that seamlessly integrated all our products and services, providing advertisers and publishers with a comprehensive solution. Through collaborative efforts and cross-functional teamwork, we successfully launched this unified platform, marking a significant milestone for GreedyGame. This initiative not only strengthened our brand identity but also enhanced user satisfaction and engagement.

Insights

We discovered other innovative opportunities to monetise apps/ games leading us to come up with two novel products- Immersive Ads and Offerwall Ads.
